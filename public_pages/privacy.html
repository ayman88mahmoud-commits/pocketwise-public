PocketWise Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-17

PocketWise is a local-first personal wallet and cash planning app. It helps you manually plan and track accounts, wallets, credit cards, budgets, income, obligations, recurring bills, and transactions.

Data You Enter

PocketWise stores information you choose to enter into the app. This may include:

  • Account and wallet names.
  • Account and wallet balances.
  • Credit card names, credit limits, and opening outstanding balances.
  • Expected income.
  • Obligations and recurring obligations.
  • Budgets.
  • Transactions and wallet activity entered or managed in the app.
  • Categories, subcategories, notes, labels, and merchant names.
  • Manual backup files that you choose to export.

Local Storage

Your PocketWise data is stored locally on your device using app-local storage. In this build, the developer does not operate a server that receives or stores your PocketWise household finance data.

No Bank Login or Payment Processing

PocketWise does not connect to banks, card issuers, payroll providers, payment networks, or financial institutions.

PocketWise does not process payments, move money, pay bills, initiate transfers, or import transactions from real financial accounts.

PocketWise does not ask for or collect bank usernames or passwords, card numbers, CVV codes, PINs, one-time passwords, security codes, or payroll credentials.

PocketWise does not include AI receipt scanning, automatic receipt reading, or automatic transaction extraction in this build.

Manual Backup, Export, and Import

PocketWise supports manual backup export and import. Exported backup files may contain sensitive household finance data. You choose when to export a backup and where to save or share it.

You are responsible for protecting exported backup files. If you save or share a backup through iCloud Drive, another cloud storage provider, email, messaging, external storage, or another app, that service's terms and privacy practices may apply.

Importing a backup may replace current app data after confirmation. Keep backup files secure and only import files you trust.

iCloud Sync

iCloud Sync is not enabled in this build. Manual backup export/import is the supported backup method for this build.

If iCloud Sync is enabled in a future build, this privacy policy should be updated before release to explain what data may sync through the user's Apple iCloud account.

Analytics, Ads, Tracking, and Sale of Data

This build of PocketWise does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

PocketWise does not sell user data.

Support

For support, contact ayman88mahmoud@icloud.com.

Do not send card numbers, CVV codes, PINs, bank passwords, one-time passwords, or sensitive backup files to support.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if PocketWise changes how it stores data, adds iCloud Sync, adds support uploads, adds analytics, or adds other external services.
