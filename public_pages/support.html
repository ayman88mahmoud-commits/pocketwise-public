PocketWise Support

PocketWise is a personal wallet and cash planning app for manually tracking household accounts, wallets, credit cards, budgets, income, obligations, recurring bills, and transactions.

No Bank Connection

PocketWise does not connect to banks, card issuers, payroll providers, payment networks, or financial institutions.

PocketWise does not process payments, move money, pay bills, or import transactions from bank accounts.

PocketWise will never ask for your bank password, card number, CVV, PIN, or one-time password.

PocketWise does not include AI receipt scanning or automatic transaction extraction in this build.

Backup, Export, and Import

Use the app's Data Backup screen to export or import a backup file.

Before major changes, export a fresh backup and store it somewhere secure.

Backup files can contain sensitive household finance data. Protect exported backup files and only share them if you intentionally decide it is safe.

When importing a backup, review the confirmation carefully because import can replace current app data.

iCloud Sync

iCloud Sync is not available in this build. For this build, use manual backup export/import as the supported backup method.

Troubleshooting

  • Force quit and reopen PocketWise.
  • Confirm your device has enough storage.
  • Check whether an item is planned, expected, unpaid, paid, or received before treating it as actual cash movement.
  • Export a fresh backup before importing or making major changes.
  • Restart the iPhone if the app or Xcode/debugger state appears stuck during development testing.
